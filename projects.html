  • PowerHour Pro

    https://market.android.com/details?id=gilday.android.powerhour

    PowerHour Pro is my experiment in the Android marketplace. After some experience with a G1, I fell in love with the platform and wanted to jump on the Android train. At the time, I couldn't justify the extra expense of a smart phone so I decided to make an application that will pay for the phone. PowerHour Pro is that application. A power hour is a drinking game my roommates and I played in college. It consists of gathering around a stereo and drinking about a shot of beer every minute for an hour. The stereo plays 60 one minute clips, and when the song changes, the participants take their drink. Inspired by an iTunes plugin, iPowerhour, I decided to bring this capability to Android as my experiment in mobile development. Further plans for this app: Open Source it?
