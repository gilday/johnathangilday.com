CERDEC Software Development Workshop

I took an opportunity to speak to my colleagues at CERDEC's Software Development Workshop. I spoke about Test Driven Development; a software development process I have grown fond of using. After a quick talk about TDD, its benefits, and what the process looks like, I demonstrated to the audience a contrived example where I added a new feature to an API the TDD way. The US Army is notoriously guilty of death by PowerPoint, so I took a page from Jesse Dee and tried to make my slides stand out.
